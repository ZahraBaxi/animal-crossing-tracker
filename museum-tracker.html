what things mean
leaving
leaving next month — available now but won't be after this month ends. Catch it soon!
new
new this month — wasn't available last month. Just arrived!
now
catchable right now — active at your current time of day based on your device clock.
M J F
month pills — shown on collection cards. Rust/filled = this month. Green = available. Faded = not available that month. year-round = active all year.
⏱ now button — filters the list to only critters you can catch at this exact hour.

show caught — reveals already-ticked critters (hidden by default to keep the list clean).

🟠 wheat row highlight — catchable right now and not yet collected.
